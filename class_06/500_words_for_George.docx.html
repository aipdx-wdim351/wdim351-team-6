Team 6 [Awesome]: Project Plan

Design Overview:

The new and totally awesome Hollywood Fitness website will have a brand new site design and web architecture using a consistent header, navigation, and general look & feel. During the process of design ideation, we will incorporate Hollywood Fitness' logo and current color scheme (red, white, grey, and black) into our comps. Our goal in formulating this new structure will be to create rich opportunities for your visitors to be led to various call-to-actions and other engaging material. This way, the path to your most valuable content is streamlined to your customers making it easier for them and more profitable for you. A win for everyone!

Technology

Hollywood Fitness will continue to reside on the WordPress Framework written with the latest HTML5 coding standards with fallback support for older browsers. We will implement a responsive solution effectively enabling your customers to easily view your website on their desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. To really set you ahead of the game and provide an added level of speed and security, we’ll position your site behind the CloudFlare DNS level caching system. Not only will this provide your website an appreciated speed boost, but it will also provide you access to a global firewall filtration system; blocking known threats before they ever hit your site.

Services Provided

We will have a few new sweet services for our good man George on this new design:

  1. We plan to give the members video functionality. With this, members will have access to upload their own videos, or comment on other. Admin users will have access to allow or reject any videos and comments that are deemed inappropriate.
  2. We will have included social media channels like Facebook & Twitter on the site.
  3. A new image and content slider for the homepage.
  4. A new photo gallery for users to get a feel for the gym atmosphere and get pumped! (will require a photo shoot).
  5. Review section for easy sharing of content and comments.
  6. Link with Google/Bing Webmaster Tools
  7. Content consolidation.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

It’s very important that your website and it’s content be indexed properly by search engines like Google and Bing. We will configure your platform to auto-generate the required sitemap.xml file and notify the search engines of the update. We’ll take this a step further by creating you Google & Bing Webmaster Tool accounts which will allow us to submit your website to their crawlers. It’s sorta like us telling Google or Bing “Hey, take a look over here for my stuff!”

If possible, we’ll attempt to correct the issue with your old Google results, provided we gain the correct level of access (and you had the access to begin with).

Security

Security is extremely important on the web, and WordPress is no exception. Being the most popular CMS platform on the planet, many WordPress specific threats exist. We will take the measures to ensure your data is safe and backed up. Advanced safety techniques will also be employed which will hide critical information from potential hackers or bots, limiting your vulnerability dramatically. More security options are available upon specific request (cost more).
